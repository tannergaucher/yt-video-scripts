Matusyni Varenyky

Welcome to Lviv, a city where culinary traditions come alive.

Today, we're taking you on a delightful journey to Матусині Вареники (Matusyni Varenyky), a Ukrainian restaurant that will transport your taste buds to a world of flavors.

Stepping inside Матусині Вареники is like stepping into a cozy Ukrainian home, where generations-old recipes and warm hospitality create an unforgettable dining experience.

Indulge in their signature dish, вареники (varenyky), lovingly handmade with the finest ingredients.

From the classic картопля з сиром (potato and cheese) to unique fillings like гриби (mushrooms) and капуста (cabbage), each bite is a celebration of Ukrainian culinary heritage.

But it's not just about the varenyky; it's the atmosphere that makes Матусині Вареники truly special. The rustic decor, combined with the warmth of the staff, creates a sense of belonging that will make you feel like part of the family.

As you savor the delicious flavors, you'll discover that Матусині Вареники is more than just a restaurant—it's a celebration of Ukrainian culture, tradition, and the love that goes into every dish.
