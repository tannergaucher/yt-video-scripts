Welcome to Lviv, a city where history and gastronomy intertwine.

Today, we're venturing into the heart of the city to uncover the secrets of Криївка (Kryivka), a unique Ukrainian restaurant that pays homage to the spirit of the resistance.

Stepping into Криївка is like stepping back in time. The dimly lit corridors, underground setting, and hidden entrance will transport you to the days of the Ukrainian Insurgent Army.

Indulge in hearty Ukrainian cuisine, enjoying dishes such as борщ (borscht), картопля по-селянськи (country-style potatoes), and вареники (varenyky) — traditional favorites that sustained the brave fighters.

But the experience at Криївка doesn't end with the food. Immerse yourself in the rich history as you explore the rooms adorned with artifacts, wartime memorabilia, and hidden surprises.

Be on the lookout for secret passageways, codes, and interactive elements that will transport you into the world of Ukrainian resistance fighters.

While enjoying the authentic flavors and embracing the historical ambiance, take a moment to reflect on the bravery and resilience of those who fought for Ukraine's freedom.

So, whether you're a history enthusiast or simply seeking a unique dining experience, make sure to visit Криївка and immerse yourself in the spirit of the resistance.

Come and experience the flavors, delve into history, and pay tribute to the heroes at Криївка — where Ukrainian cuisine and wartime stories come together.
