Welcome to Lviv, a city where tradition and taste collide.

Today, we're taking you on an extraordinary journey to experience the mouthwatering flavors of Varenyky Tut, the Ukrainian restaurant that will leave you craving for more.

Stepping into Varenyky Tut is like entering a culinary paradise, where the aroma of freshly made varenyky fills the air.

Indulge your senses with their wide variety of varenyky, the beloved Ukrainian dumplings that come in an array of fillings, from traditional картопля (potato) and сир (cheese) to innovative combinations that will surprise and delight.

But it's not just the varenyky that sets Varenyky Tut apart. It's the passion and dedication behind every dish that makes it truly special.

Their commitment to using locally sourced, high-quality ingredients ensures that each bite is a burst of authentic flavors.

As you savor their varenyky, you'll also be enchanted by the warm and inviting atmosphere, reminiscent of a cozy Ukrainian cottage.

Whether you're a seasoned varenyky connoisseur or new to this delightful dish, Varenyky Tut offers a culinary experience that will leave you wanting more.

So, when you find yourself in Lviv, make sure to visit Varenyky Tut and indulge in the authentic flavors of Ukraine.
