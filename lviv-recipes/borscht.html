Today, we're making a classic Ukrainian dish - Борщ

You'll need beets, cabbage, carrots, potatoes, onions, garlic, beans, beef or vegetable broth, tomato paste, vinegar, and optional herbs and spices.

Finely chop the onions and mince the garlic. Grate the beets and carrots. Slice the cabbage and cube the potatoes.

In a large pot, heat some oil and sauté the onions until translucent. Add the garlic and sauté for another minute until fragrant.

Add the grated beets and carrots to the pot and cook until they soften and release their vibrant colors. Stir in the tomato paste and cook for a few more minutes.

Pour in the broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for about 15-20 minutes.

Add the sliced cabbage, cubed potatoes, and beans to the pot. Continue simmering until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have melded together.

Season the борщ with salt, pepper, and vinegar to taste.

Serve the борщ with a dollop of sour cream and a sprinkle of fresh herbs.

And there you have it! A steaming bowl of delicious Ukrainian борщ
