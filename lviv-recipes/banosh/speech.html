Quick and Easy Banosh Recipe - Taste the Ukrainian Delight!

Hey there, foodies! Welcome to our channel!

Today, we have a mouthwatering Ukrainian delight for you. Get ready to learn how to make the delicious Банош in just 60 seconds! Let's dive in!

First, grab a bowl of fine cornmeal. It's the star ingredient that gives Банош its unique texture and taste!

Add some water and mix it well until you get a smooth consistency. Trust me, this will be worth it!

Now, let's heat it up on medium heat while stirring continuously. We want it to thicken up like a creamy porridge.

Next, let's enhance the flavors with some delicious ingredients. Add a generous knob of butter and mix it in.

Sprinkle in some shredded cheese and give it a good stir. This will make our Банош extra creamy and cheesy!

For a burst of freshness, add finely chopped dill and mix it well. The dill adds a lovely aroma and flavor to the dish!

To take our Банош to the next level, let's add some crunchy goodness. Crush some pork cracklings and sprinkle them over the Банош.

Finally, it's time to serve our Банош. Scoop it into bowls and top it off with a dollop of sour cream.

There you have it! A delectable and comforting Ukrainian Банош with the added savory touch of pork cracklings. Go ahead and dig in!
